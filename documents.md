## Definitions

**RO Tools** = Links, tools, document generators, branded material creation. Anything an operator generates, references, or downloads.

**Mission Control** = Live store functions. Numbers, Jolt, Homebase, scheduling — the day-to-day tools an individual store needs to operate.

**Internal** = Documents used within the company (training, checklists, internal forms). Use JM Valley logo.

**External/Customer-Facing** = Documents given to customers (flyers, leave-behinds, marketing). Use Jersey Mike's corporate branding.
